## VoiceTube

```dataview
table
	without ID
	file.link as name,
	file.mtime as "last edit",
	file.folder as folder,
	status
where note_type != "MOC"
and file.ctime >= (date(now) - dur("1 weeks"))
and contains(source_url, "voicetube")
sort file.mtime desc
```
